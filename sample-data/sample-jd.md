# Sample Job Description

## Position

Sales Operations Specialist

## Job Responsibilities

- Support sales team performance tracking and reporting
- Analyze sales pipeline, conversion rate, and customer follow-up status
- Maintain CRM data accuracy and improve sales operation workflows
- Coordinate with sales, compliance, product, and internal operations teams
- Prepare weekly and monthly sales performance reports
- Identify process bottlenecks and propose optimization solutions
- Assist in sales training materials, SOP documentation, and internal communication

## Requirements

- 2+ years of experience in sales support, sales operations, business operations, or financial services
- Strong communication and cross-functional coordination skills
- Experience with data analysis, reporting, or dashboard tools
- Familiar with Excel or Google Sheets
- Able to organize complex information into clear workflows or SOPs
- Detail-oriented, proactive, and comfortable working with sales teams

## Preferred Qualifications

- Experience in banking, fintech, SaaS, or B2B sales environments
- Experience with CRM systems
- Ability to use AI tools to improve workflow efficiency
- Basic understanding of sales funnel, conversion rate, and performance metrics
